USER

Business analysts, researchers, entrepreneurs, students, and decision-makers
who need to stay updated with latest industry trends, emerging technologies, and market insights.

PROBLEM

Tracking and analyzing industry trends manually requires extensive research across multiple sources such as news websites, blogs, reports, and online platforms.
This involves collecting scattered information and synthesizing it into meaningful insights, which is time-consuming, inefficient, and often inconsistent.

CURRENT SOLUTION

Manual research using search engines, reading articles, reports, and industry blogs, and compiling insights into documents or presentations.
Organizations may also rely on market research firms or analysts for trend reports.


LIMITATIONS OF CURRENT APPROACH

Time-consuming (hours or days of manual effort)
Information overload from multiple unstructured sources
Lack of real-time insights
Human bias in interpreting trends
Expensive when relying on professional analysts or reports

WHY AN AI AGENT IS NEEDED

A simple chatbot is not sufficient because:
It cannot continuously fetch and analyze real-time data from multiple sources
It cannot break down the task into research, analysis, and summarization steps
It lacks the ability to reason, refine, and validate insights autonomously

An agentic AI system overcomes these limitations by combining:
Live data retrieval
Task decomposition into specialized agents
Autonomous reasoning and structured output generation

SOLUTION

Industry Trend AI Agent — a multi-agent system that:
Collects real-time data from multiple online sources
Uses Agent 1 to gather and filter relevant industry information
Uses Agent 2 to analyze patterns and identify emerging trends
Uses Agent 3 to generate structured summaries and insights
Uses Agent 4 to refine and validate output quality

TECH STACK

Search Tool: Tavily API
Frontend & Deployment: Streamlit (Streamlit Cloud)
Language: Python 3
